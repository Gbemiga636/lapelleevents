About Us

Meticulous planning. Distinctive style.

Lapelle Events is a full‑service planning and management company delivering bespoke experiences across Nigeria. As seasoned planners, we handle every detail—from the first idea to the final applause—so your event feels effortless, personal, and unforgettable.

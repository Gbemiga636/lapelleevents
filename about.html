Who we are

Full-service event planning across Nigeria

Lapelle Events is a full-service event planning and management company capable of managing events across Nigeria. As experienced event planners, we can handle every single aspect of your event details from start to finish. Your event will be a one-of-a-kind memory as we attend to the details and add our creative touch. Our service is uniquely planned and customized to your satisfaction.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To design and deliver effortless, exceptional events that reflect our clients’ stories, priorities and brand — every detail considered, every moment choreographed.

Vision: To be Nigeria’s most trusted event partner — recognized for creativity, operational excellence, and measurable client value.

Client-first: your goals guide design and delivery.
Reliable & timely: detailed timelines and contingency planning.
